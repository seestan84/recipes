Ingredients
4-5 cloves of garlic
1 stack spring onions
2 slices of Spam, cubed
2 cups of day-old rice
1 beaten egg
salt

Instructions
1. Quickly fry beaten egg, and set aside.
2. Saute garlic until slightly golden. Add Spam, fry until edges are crispy.
3. Add the rice, egg and salt. Combine well.
4. Add the spring onions just before serving.
